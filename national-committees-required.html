NATIONAL COMMITTEES REQUIREMENTS OF THE INTERNATIONAL BLOCKCHAIN OLYMPIAD

National Committees requirements of the registration informations

R1 - Minimum Viable Registration

Last day for revisions: Sun 28 Jun 23:59 GMT

  • Representative Country
  • Representative School
  • Team Name

R2 - Additional Information

Last day for revisions: Mon 29 Jun 23:59 GMT

  • Project Name
  • Project description (3-4 sentences)
  • Team's Virtual Room (Zoom Meeting ID/pwd/URL)
  • Team Member Names (max 6)
  • Team Member Photos (individual)
  • Team Member Biography (2-4 sentences)
  • Team Member Schools
  • Team Member interested to be a panelist? (Y/N)

R3 - Presentation Material

Last day for revisions: Tue 30 Jun 23:59 GMT

  • Posterboard URL
  • Pitch Deck URL

R4 - Presentation Material II

Last day for revisions: Wed 1 Jul 23:59 GMT

  • Pitch Video (URL or upload)
  • Any other interactive component (URL and/or upload)

R5 - Jury Nomination

Last day for revisions: Thu 2 Jul 23:59 GMT

  • Juror's Name
  • Juror's Title/Role/Position
  • Juror's institution
  • Juror's Photo
  • Juror's Biography (3-4 sentences)

R6 - VIP Registration

Last day for revisions: none (asap)

  • VIP's Name
  • VIP's Title/Role/Position
  • VIP's institution
  • VIP's Photo - optional
  • VIP's Biography (3-4 sentences) - optional


And this is what we do with each registration interaction, by type:

R1 - send back Team ID and Contestants' Guidebook
R2 - send back Team ID (to re-confirm) and Teammates' badge ID(s)
R3 - send back Team's Slack Channel (to re-confirm)
R4 - send back Team's Slack Channel (to re-confirm)
R5 - send back Juror's badge ID(s) and Jurors' Guidebook
R6 - send back VIP's badge ID(s) and Visitors' Guidebook
