Project Description

This International Blockchain Olympiad Whitepaper proposes using blockchain technology to create a blockchain-based interoperable system to track and trace the cross-border movement of pharmaceuticals and relevant regulatory approvals for those pharmaceuticals (“Whitepaper”). This Whitepaper is unique to other track and trace projects in that it 1) proposes a multi-signature protocol to authenticate multiple party certifications necessary for the movement of pharmaceuticals, and 2) addresses the legal aspects and added issues relating to the cross-border movement of pharmaceuticals.
