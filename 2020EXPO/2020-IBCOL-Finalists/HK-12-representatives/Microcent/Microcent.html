Project Description

The Internet has made it possible to offer microcredit without the need for a bank. For instance, P2P loans brought a shift in the credit system by offering low-interest rates and simple requirements for borrower’s credit evaluation when compared to traditional banks. Though the P2P solution turned out to be revolutionary by bringing in significant changes from the traditional banking industry, the solution still has major structural issues that need to be solved. First, the P2P solution aimed to eliminate banks as the third party; however, the company backing the P2P platform still acts like a third party, controlling most of the platform decisions. Second, P2P platforms lack transparency in onboarding the borrower and credit risk assessment. For instance, many P2P platforms were sued in the past for selling bad loans to the lenders as the lenders solely make the lending decision based on the credit score rated by these platforms. Third, lenders' contracts are restricted to a specified term, so they cannot exit the contract and get their amount back until the expiration date. Finally, P2P pays significantly low returns to the lender since the majority of the income from interest rate goes to business operation costs and loan origination fees; hence, the lender participating in a P2P lending platform might not be compensated enough for the risk taken.

MicroCent is an attempt to nurture the existing microcredit industry by utilizing indigenous machine learning algorithms and the disruptive attribute of blockchain technology that bring the highest standards of evaluation, transparency, and cost-efficiency. MicroCent uses machine-learning algorithms to evaluate the borrowers in real-time and generate ‘Block Passport’ that serves as a decentralized ID to receive loans instantly from a tradable blockchain network. The Block Passport that MicroCent provides for borrowers is global, borderless, mobile, well-rounded, objective, and adaptive to lenders' preferences. Once the borrower with ‘Block Passport’, places the loan request, neither MicroCent nor any other party can manipulate, suspend, or stop the loan.

Each loan request from the borrower will be divided into multiple smart contracts, and each contract can be traded by the lenders in the blockchain until the expiration date. This creates flexibility for the lenders to diversify risk and exit contracts by trading the contracts they hold if they wish to receive payment immediately. Unlike traditional P2P platforms, MicroCent follows ‘The Best Execution’ mechanism, which makes the platform highly transparent and liquid. Lenders portfolio is automatically maintained by an algorithm embedded in the blockchain that finds the best possible smart contract based on the lender's risk attribute, and this matching rule is transparent and universal for all the lenders in the blockchain network. While maintaining liquidity for the borrower to immediately access funds & lenders to exit contracts, the best execution mechanism ensures that neither MicroCent nor any other party can take advantage of the platform. MicroCent is neither a P2P lending solution nor a crypto lending solution. The lending structure of MicroCent is unique to the microcredit/lending industry.
