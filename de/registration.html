Deadlines

(Re)submissions

You may submit as many files and revisions as you like. Please clearly reference files in main paper if submitting multiple files.

Project Submission Grading Distribution

Whitepaper (40%): a project is described in a document. There are no limits on length or formatting. Whitepaper(s) may be multilingual, but English must be one of the languages present. Whitepaper file(s) must be in PDF format.

Posterboard (40%): a project has a research poster or science poster to showcase at the IBCOL Finals. Poster board content may be multilingual, but English must be one of the languages present. Posterboard file may be in PDF format or as a Figma file.

Presentation (20%): a project may be invited to be presented; aim to finish by 10 minutes, hard-stop at 12 minutes. Presentation content and speech may be multilingual, but English must be one of the languages. Presentation files will be presented in 16:9 on either Microsoft PowerPoint or Google Sheets; a PDF backup is recommended.
