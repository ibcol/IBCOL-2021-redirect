Project Manager

We are currently seeking a Project Manager to join the team. The Project Manager will manage projects to support HKBCS’ objectives.

Primary responsibilities:

  • Manage projects surrounding HKBCS, such as academic and industry partnerships, DappCamp Blockchain Academy projects, and so forth.
  • Manage the HKBCS team in ensuring that projects are conducted in a timely manner with high quality
  • Provide support to all stakeholders relevant to the organization and the projects
  • Prepare proposals, manage events, and create programs pertaining to the organization

Qualifications:

  • Interested in learning more about Blockchain technology
  • Proficient in English (Cantonese and Mandarin a plus)
  • Strong communication skills
  • Knowledge of legal or financial industry a plus

*This is an unpaid, volunteer position*
