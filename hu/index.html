About IBCOL

The HUngary Blockchain OLympiad (HU-BCOL) invites current and recent students to solve real-world problems with decentralized applications, built with blockchain or distributed ledger technology. Top scoring projects proceed to the International BlockChain OLympiad (IBCOL) in July 2020.
