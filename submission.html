SUBMIT A PROJECT TO THE INTERNATIONAL BLOCKCHAIN OLYMPIAD

Project Submissions

To qualify for the International Blockchain Olympiad, participants must regsiter themselves with the IBCOL Registrar’s Office, and go through the process of their local blockchain olympiad committees. However, all final teams must register with IBCOL committee.

SUBMIT VIA FORM SUBMIT VIA EMAIL

Submit Project via Email

Registration by form is preferred but if you are unable to access Google from your internet connection, please send an email to PMO@ibcol.org with the subject [xx] Project Submission, where xx is the ISO 3166 code for your country (for re-submission or updates, please reply to the same email thread) with the following information:

A. Contact Information


1. Contact Email Address *
Same as the one registered at bit.ly/ibcol-registrar

2. Contact Telephone Number *
Same as the one registered at bit.ly/ibcol-registrar

B. Team Particulars


1. Team Name
Optional — in lieu of a Team Name, a Team Name shall be suggested or assigned by IBCOL.

2. Team Member Email Addresses (Registrar)
Same as the one registered at bit.ly/ibcol-registrar (separate emails by semi-colon; leave empty if single-person team)

3. Team Member Phone Numbers (Registrar)
Same as the one registered at bit.ly/ibcol-registrar (separate phone number by semi-colon; leave empty if single-person team)

4. Representative Institution *
In cases where only one institution could be displayed, select the institution of one of your teammates.

5. Representative Country/Territory *
Country or territory in which your representative institution is located.

C. Project Particulars


1. Project Name *
This can also be a concise description of the project

2. Project Abstract *
This can also be a concise description of the project

3. Problem & Solution (30%)
The project addresses an appropriate problem properly

4. Market & Partners (10%)
The project includes the appropriate collaboration properly

5. Competition & Risks (20%)
The project is transparent about its potential vulnerabilities

6. Architecture & Governance (30%)
The project has appropriate design fundamentals

7. Revenue & Distribution (10%)
The project has value and can be brought into reality

8. Upload Whitepaper
Don’t forget the attachment!

9. Upload Posterboard Presentation
Don’t forget the attachment!

10. Upload Pitch Presentation
Don’t forget the attachment!

11. [Alternative] Whitepaper URL
Include https://

12. [Alternative] Posterboard Presentation URL
Include https://

13. [Alternative] Pitch Presentation URL
Include https://
