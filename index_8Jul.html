Visitor Registration

IBCOL 2020 is an annual event that challenges students worldwide to propose realistic solutions using distributed ledger technology. We expect a diverse representation with contestants from Bangladesh, Canada, China, Hong Kong, Korea, Mongolia, Netherlands, Pakistan, Philippines, Poland, Taiwan, Turkey, United Kingdom, United States, Vietnam.

Due to the difficulty in international travel and in the spirit of fair play, the IBCOL Finals are held entirely online while accommodating participants from timezones from the Eastern Australian to Western American continents.
